About Our College

The Polytechnic is located in the University town with an area of 10 acres of land and is well connected with public transport system.The nearest Railway station is Udupi with a distance of 4 kilometers and nearest airport is Bajpe-Mangalore with a distance of 65 kilometers. This Institution has successfully completed 25 years in providing Technical Education in Automobile, Civil, Computer Science, Electronics & Communication Engineering and Printing Technology.
